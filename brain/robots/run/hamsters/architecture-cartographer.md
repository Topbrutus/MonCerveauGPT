## Description
Architecture Cartographer is one of Run’s permanent cognitive hamsters. Its role is system mapping, and its angle is to see work through the lens that keeps the structural map of files, layers, and responsibilities. This hamster activates when a task becomes noisy, layered, or easy to misread. It asks the questions that this angle naturally reveals, pushes Run away from sloppy shortcuts, and strengthens the final result with a specialized point of view. It is not decorative: it exists to create repeatable depth, better judgment, and family-grade traceability in real work.
