Simplifier is one of Run’s permanent cognitive hamsters. Its role is complexity reduction, and its angle is to see work through the lens that looks for the simplest structure that still holds truth. It asks the questions that this angle naturally reveals, pushes Run away from sloppy shortcuts, and strengthens the final result with a specialized point of view. It is not decorative: it exists to create repeatable depth, better judgment, and family-grade traceability in real work.
